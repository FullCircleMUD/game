{# ── Jurisdictional Restrictions ── #}

Jurisdictional Restrictions

The gold redemption feature is offered exclusively to players accessing Full Circle MUD from an eligible jurisdiction as determined by our geo-detection systems, and whose account login history is consistent with access from an eligible jurisdiction as set out in the Eligible Jurisdictions List.

Residents of the United States of America (including all US territories), all European Economic Area member states, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, Canada, Japan, South Korea, and any other jurisdiction in which Full Circle MUD's gold redemption feature would constitute an unlicensed or regulated financial service, are not eligible for gold redemption.

By creating an account, players in restricted jurisdictions acknowledge and agree that:

  1. The gold redemption feature is not available to them and they will not attempt to access it by any means including the use of virtual private networks or other location-masking technologies;
  2. Gold earned in-game by players in restricted jurisdictions represents in-game progression only and does not constitute a financial instrument, payment instrument, stablecoin, electronic money token, or enforceable claim against Full Circle MUD or any related entity;
  3. Full Circle MUD has not solicited, marketed, or offered any financial product to players in restricted jurisdictions;
  4. Any attempt to circumvent jurisdictional restrictions constitutes a material breach of these Terms and may result in immediate account suspension and forfeiture of any pending redemption requests.
{# ── Location Determination ── #}

Location Determination

Full Circle MUD determines user location automatically using IP geolocation technology for regulatory compliance purposes. This determination is made at the network edge before page content is rendered and cannot be modified through browser settings, language preferences, or other client-side means.

Full Circle MUD records the detected jurisdiction for each login session. These records are retained for a minimum of three years and constitute Full Circle MUD's documentation of reasonable efforts to restrict the gold redemption feature to eligible jurisdictions.

Players who believe their location has been determined incorrectly may submit a documented review request to compliance@fcmud.world. Full Circle MUD cannot accommodate requests to change detected location without satisfactory documentation, and no self-service location change mechanism is provided.

Prohibition on Circumvention

Players are prohibited from using virtual private networks, proxy services, or any other technology or method designed to misrepresent their physical location for the purpose of accessing features unavailable in their actual jurisdiction. Such use constitutes:

  1. A material breach of these Terms of Service;
  2. A misrepresentation made to Full Circle MUD's compliance systems; and
  3. Assumption by the player of all legal responsibility arising from any regulatory non-compliance in their actual jurisdiction.

Full Circle MUD accepts no liability for any loss, regulatory consequence, or legal exposure arising from a player's misrepresentation of their physical location.

{# ── Nature of In-Game Gold ── #}

Nature of In-Game Gold

For players in restricted jurisdictions, in-game gold represents in-game progression earned through gameplay activity only. It does not constitute a financial instrument, a claim on any reserve, or an entitlement to any monetary value. Full Circle MUD makes no representation to restricted-jurisdiction players regarding the monetary value of in-game gold and has not offered any financial product to such players.

For players in eligible jurisdictions, the nature of in-game gold, the reserve backing, and the redemption mechanism are described on the Costs & Transparency and Redemption pages.

{# ── Alpha and Testing Phases ── #}

Alpha and Testing Phases

During pre-alpha and alpha phases operating on XRPL testnet, all in-game gold and items are test assets with no monetary value. Test phase assets are not transferable to the mainnet economy and carry no entitlement to any reserve, redemption, or monetary value. Players who participated in testnet phases have no claim arising from that participation in respect of mainnet assets or the mainnet economy.
