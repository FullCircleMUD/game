Privacy Policy

Status: Draft — pending legal review before mainnet launch. This policy describes how Full Circle MUD collects, uses, and retains data about players. By creating an account you agree to the practices described here.

{# ── What We Collect ── #}

What We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  1. Account data — your chosen username, the XRPL wallet address you authenticated with, account creation date, and character names associated with your account.
  2. Session records — a hashed representation of your IP address (SHA-256 one-way hash; the raw IP address is never stored) and the detected country code for each login session.
  3. Game activity — session start and end times, in-game economic activity (gold and resource transfers, crafting, trading), and quest and progression data.

We do not collect payment card data, passwords (authentication uses cryptographic wallet signatures), or sensitive personal information beyond what is listed above.

{# ── IP Address Hashing ── #}

IP Address Hashing

Full Circle MUD does not store raw IP addresses. When your IP address is used for geo-detection, it is immediately hashed using SHA-256 (a one-way cryptographic function) and only the hash is retained. The hash allows us to demonstrate that geo-detection was performed on a given session without storing any data that could be used to identify your network location after the fact.

The detected country code (a two-letter ISO code such as "PY" or "US") is stored alongside the hash as the operative compliance record.

{# ── How We Use Data ── #}

How We Use Data

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  1. Game operation — session and activity data is used to run the game, calculate the in-game economy, and maintain account state.
  2. Transparency reporting — aggregated, anonymised economic data may be published in game economy reports. No individual player data is included in these reports.
  3. Fraud and abuse prevention — account and session data may be used to investigate suspected violations of the Terms of Service, including attempted abuse of game systems.

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

{# ── Retention Periods ── #}

Retention Periods

We retain different categories of data for different periods:

Data Category Retention Period Reason
Account data (username, wallet address) Duration of account + 3 years Account management, dispute resolution
Session records (IP hash, country code) 3 years from each session Fraud prevention, abuse detection
In-game economic activity logs 3 years Economy audit trail, dispute resolution

After the applicable retention period, data is deleted or anonymised such that it can no longer be associated with an individual account.

{# ── Your Rights ── #}

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights regarding your personal data. Because our player base consists of users in jurisdictions outside the EEA/UK where GDPR applies, GDPR does not govern this policy. However, we respect the following rights for all players:

  1. Access — you may request a summary of the personal data we hold about your account.
  2. Correction — if your account data is inaccurate, you may request a correction.
  3. Deletion — you may request deletion of your account. Note that certain data required for operational purposes (session records, economic activity logs) cannot be deleted before the end of the applicable retention period.
  4. Objection to processing — you may object to processing of your data for purposes other than those listed in this policy.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at compliance@fcmud.world. We will respond within 30 days.

{# ── Third Parties ── #}

Third Parties

We share data with the following categories of third parties:

  1. Infrastructure providers — hosting and database services that store game data on our behalf, under data processing agreements.
  2. XRPL network — on-chain transactions (wallet addresses, token transfers) are recorded on the XRP Ledger, which is a public blockchain. Wallet addresses and transaction details are publicly visible.

We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

{# ── Contact ── #}

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy, data access requests, correction requests, or deletion requests should be directed to compliance@fcmud.world.
