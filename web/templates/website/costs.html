A subscription is required to play Full Circle MUD.

{# ── Why a Subscription ── #}

Why a Subscription?

Most games monetise through microtransactions, loot boxes, or selling power. We don't. Full Circle MUD uses a simple monthly subscription because it's the only model that lets us build the game we want to build — sustainably, without selling power or player data.

Your subscription is what sustains the game. A flat monthly fee keeps the economics simple — no microtransactions, no loot boxes, no pay-to-win. The game stands on its own merits.

{# ── What You Get ── #}

What Does a Subscription Get You?

Your subscription gives you full access to the game:

  • Unlimited play — log in any time, on any character
  • Full access to the crafting economy, dungeons, and world content
  • AI-driven NPCs that remember you and react to your reputation
  • On-chain item ownership — your gear is yours, stored on the XRPL
  • All future content updates included at no extra cost

There is nothing else to buy. No premium currency, no battle passes, no cosmetic shop. One subscription, the whole game.

{# ── Pricing Tiers ── #}

Pricing

Alpha: $2/month

While the game is in alpha and the economy is running on testnet, subscriptions are just $2 per month. This covers our running costs and helps fund ongoing development.

Post Go-Live: $10/month

When the game goes live and the economy moves to XRPL mainnet, the subscription moves to $10 per month. This covers hosting, infrastructure, LLM services, and ongoing development.

{# ── The Game Economy ── #}

The Game Economy

Gold doesn't just flow in — it circulates. When you process resources at a mill, craft items at a forge, or pay fees at an NPC shop, you're feeding the game's gold sinks. These sinks exist to combat inflation and keep the economy healthy for everyone.

The result is a living economy where gold flows in a circle: players earn it through questing and exploration, spend it through crafting and trading, and sinks recirculate it back into the world for others to earn. NPC shop prices adjust dynamically based on supply and demand, so the economy responds to how players actually play.

{# ── Our Running Costs ── #}

Our Running Costs

We believe in full transparency. Here's what it costs to keep Full Circle MUD running:

Hosting & Infrastructure

Server hosting, database backup & management, domain and DNS services, all the infrastructure that keeps the game world running 24/7.

Blockchain Transaction Fees

Every NPC shop transaction, every player import and export, and every token issued on the XRP Ledger incurs a small transaction fee. Individually tiny, but they add up as the player base grows.

LLM Services

Our AI-driven NPCs aren't scripted chatbots — every conversation with an NPC is a real API call to a large language model. When a shopkeeper remembers your name, when a quest giver reacts to your reputation, when a boss taunts you mid-fight — that's real compute. These API costs scale with the number of active players and the depth of NPC interactions.

Development

New features, content updates, bug fixes, and the ongoing work of building a world worth living in. Full Circle MUD is an independent project built with care, not a venture-funded sprint to an exit.

Game Economy Reports

Once the game economy goes live, we'll publish periodic game economy reports covering gold circulation, player activity, and operating expenses. Full transparency — you'll always know how the game is doing.
