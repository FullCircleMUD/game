Eligible Jurisdictions

The gold redemption feature is available to players accessing Full Circle MUD from the following jurisdictions. Eligibility is determined by IP geolocation at time of access. Players in jurisdictions not listed below are not eligible for gold redemption.

This list reflects our current launch jurisdictions. Additional jurisdictions may be added before or after mainnet launch as legal review is completed for each region.

Not on This List?

If your country is not listed above, the gold redemption feature is not available to you at this time. Geo-detection is applied automatically and cannot be overridden through browser settings or location-masking technologies. See our Terms of Service for details on jurisdictional restrictions.

If you believe your location has been determined incorrectly, you may submit a documented review request to compliance@fcmud.world.
