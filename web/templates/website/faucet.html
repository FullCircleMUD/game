How It Works

  1. Set up a FakeRLUSD trust line using Step 1 above (one-time only)
  2. Enter your XRPL testnet wallet address in Step 2 and click Send
  3. 20 FakeRLUSD will be sent to your wallet from the testnet faucet
  4. Log into the game and type subscribe at the account menu
  5. Follow the prompts to sign the subscription payment in your Xaman wallet

FakeRLUSD is a testnet token with no real value. It simulates the RLUSD payments that will be used on mainnet.
