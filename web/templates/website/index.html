{# ── Pre-Alpha Notice ── #}

Pre-Alpha — Adventure at Your Own Risk!

Full Circle MUD is in pre-alpha development. The game is currently running on XRPL Testnet with the economy in test mode — testnet gold and items have no monetary value and cannot be transferred to the mainnet economy. We'll move to mainnet and the economy will go live when the game is ready for beta release. You're welcome to test the import and export of NFTs and game tokens to your testnet wallet and let us know if you can see ways to improve the experience.

We'll do our best to let early adventurers keep what they earn once we go live, but during the pre-alpha and alpha phases we reserve the right to rebalance, wipe, or change anything in the game as development requires. Think of it as scouting ahead — the real treasure is the stories you'll have when the world opens for good.

{# ── Feature Cards ── #}
Player Economy

If a shopkeeper sells it, a player harvested or crafted it. Farmers, miners, smiths, alchemists — every link in the supply chain is a real player.

Own Your Loot

Items are NFTs. Resources are fungible tokens. Gold is an issued currency on the XRPL. Export anything to your own wallet whenever you want. They're yours.

Living NPCs

AI-driven mobs remember past fights and adapt. Build a relationship with a boss — or watch it learn to counter your tactics.

Never Solved

Procedurally generated dungeons mean you explore fresh every time. No walkthroughs. No memorised paths. Every run is real.

{# ── What Kind of Game ── #}
Classic MUD, Modern Ambition

Eight classes. Twenty-four weapon types with unique mastery progressions. Six schools of magic where spells gain new abilities — not just bigger numbers — as you advance. Crafting chains from raw ore to enchanted weapons. This is a deep, text-driven game in the tradition of CircleMUD and DikuMUD.

Read the vision →
On-Chain Economy

NPC shop prices are driven by on-ledger AMMs — supply and demand, not scripts. Every asset you earn in-game is a real on-chain token you can export to your own wallet on the XRP Ledger and trade freely.
