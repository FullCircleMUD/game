{# ── Pre-Alpha Notice ── #}

Pre-Alpha — Adventure at Your Own Risk!

Full Circle MUD is in pre-alpha development on the XRPL mainnet. The game currently operates as a closed-loop system — asset export is disabled until beta. During pre-alpha and alpha, we reserve the right to remove or rewrite items at any time as needed to balance the game.

We'll endeavour to let pre-alpha and alpha players keep as much of what they earn as possible when the game goes live — however we offer no guarantee.

From beta onwards, items become permanent and export to your own wallet will be enabled. Think of playing now as scouting ahead — the real treasure is the stories you'll have when the world opens for good.

{# ── Feature Cards ── #}
Player Economy

If a shopkeeper sells it, a player harvested or crafted it. Farmers, miners, smiths, alchemists — every link in the supply chain is a real player.

Own Your Loot

Items are NFTs. Gold and resources are issued currencies on the XRPL. From beta, export anything to your own wallet. They're yours.

Living NPCs

AI-driven mobs remember past fights and adapt. Build a relationship with a boss — or watch it learn to counter your tactics.

Never Solved

Procedurally generated dungeons mean you explore fresh every time. No walkthroughs. No memorised paths. Every run is real.

{# ── What Kind of Game ── #}
Classic MUD, Modern Ambition

An expanding roster of races and classes. Over twenty weapon types with unique mastery progressions. Six schools of magic where spells gain new abilities — not just bigger numbers — as you advance. Crafting chains from raw ore to enchanted weapons. This is a deep, text-driven game in the tradition of CircleMUD and DikuMUD.

Read the vision →
On-Chain Economy

NPC shop prices are driven by on-ledger AMMs — supply and demand, not scripts. Every asset you earn in-game is a real on-chain token. From beta, you'll be able to export to your own wallet on the XRP Ledger and trade freely.
