Full Circle MUD

Eve Online meets Ultima Online, with a transparent on-chain economy.

A text-based multiplayer world where every coin, every sword, and every hide of leather is an on-chain asset you own.

{# ── The Name ── #}

Why "Full Circle"?

The name comes from the game's heritage. Full Circle MUD is inspired by the founder's teenage years spent on CircleMUD-based MUDs — games descended from DikuMUD that defined a generation of text-based multiplayer worlds. This project aims to bring those worlds full circle: the same addictive, text-driven gameplay, combined with ideas that simply weren't possible on a 56k modem in the late '90s.

It also describes the economy. Gold earned through questing becomes resources, resources become crafted items, items get traded, and gold circulates back into the world for others to earn. The full circle.

{# ── Pillars ── #}

Design Pillars

1. You Own Your Assets

Gold and resources — wheat, iron ore, moonpetal essence — are all issued currencies on the XRP Ledger. Every crafted item is an NFT — real on-chain assets managed through your Xaman wallet.

From beta, you'll be able to import assets into the game and export them to your wallet to trade freely. During pre-alpha and alpha, the game operates in closed-loop mode while we balance the economy.

2. True Custody

While you're playing, your assets sit in a game-controlled bridge wallet. From beta, you can export them to your own wallet at any time. Every ledger transaction is initiated and signed by you — the game server provides cryptographic authorisations, but never moves assets without your approval.

The result: your assets are genuinely yours.

3. A Real Economy, Not a Token Economy

Most blockchain games create a token and call it an economy. FCM builds an actual medieval economy with supply chains, crafting professions, resource scarcity, and NPC markets — all running transparently on a public ledger.

Farmers harvest wheat. Millers grind it to flour. Bakers bake bread. Miners pull ore from the ground. Smelters refine ingots. Blacksmiths forge weapons. Alchemists brew potions from rare herbs. Every step has cost, skill requirements, and market dynamics. Dozens of resource types feed into multiple crafting professions through interconnected supply chains.

NPC markets use constant-product AMMs — powered by the XRPL's native DEX — so prices respond to supply and demand. Flood the market with iron and the price drops. Corner the moonpetal supply and watch essence prices climb. The economy is emergent, not scripted.

4. Deep Gameplay First

The blockchain enables the economy, but the game stands on its own. An expanding roster of races and character classes. Over twenty weapon types, each with unique mechanics that evolve across five mastery tiers — a longsword in the hands of a Grandmaster plays fundamentally differently than in the hands of a novice. Six schools of magic with spells that gain qualitative new abilities as you advance, not just bigger numbers. Stealth, traps, reactive combat, group tactics.

This is a MUD in the tradition of the genre's best — rich, text-driven, imagination-first — with the addition that the loot you earn is truly yours on the blockchain and the economy you participate in is transparent and player-driven.

{# ── The World ── #}

The World

Full Circle MUD is a persistent multiplayer world connected via WebSocket. No downloads, no installs — connect from any client that speaks the protocol, or build your own.

Classes

Warrior, Thief, Mage, Cleric, Bard, Berserker, Paladin, and more. Each class unlocks unique skills and plays distinctly. A thief hides in shadows and picks pockets; a mage commands six schools of arcane power; a cleric channels divine healing and judgement.

Combat

D20-based resolution with initiative, advantage, reactive spells, and weapon mastery. Every weapon type — longsword, nunchaku, war hammer, longbow — has its own progression curve with abilities that unlock at each mastery tier.

Crafting

Over ten professions, each with their own unique skills and recipes. Gather raw materials, refine them through processing stations, and craft finished goods. Every step produces a real on-ledger asset. Mastery unlocks better recipes and faster production.

Magic

Six arcane schools and four divine domains. Spells scale qualitatively with mastery — Find Familiar at Basic summons a rat; at Skilled, a stealthy cat; at Expert, a flying owl; at Master, a hawk that fights alongside you. Every tier is a meaningful upgrade.

{# ── How It Works ── #}

How It Works

Connect

Link your Xaman wallet. Your wallet address is your identity. No email, no password — just a cryptographic signature that proves you are you.

Play

Explore, fight, craft, trade. While you're in the game, your assets live in a secure multisig XRPL wallet that holds everything in play. The game tracks your inventory, your bank, your equipment.

Own

Your sword is an NFT. Your gold and resources are issued currencies on the XRP Ledger. From beta, export any asset to your personal wallet and trade freely. They're yours.

{# ── Technical Foundation ── #}

Technical Foundation

Built on Evennia, a mature Python/Django MUD framework, with game assets issued natively on the XRP Ledger. A mirror database tracks all on-ledger state, ensuring the game world and the ledger always agree.

Gold is issued as a currency on the XRP Ledger by the game's issuer wallet. The vault holds all in-game assets. Our reconciliation process ensures every transfer between wallet and vault is accounted for on both sides.

One ledger. Near-zero fees. Complete player custody.
