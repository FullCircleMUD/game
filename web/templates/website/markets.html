How AMM Pricing Works

Resources in Full Circle MUD are traded through Automated Market Maker (AMM) pools at NPC shopkeepers. Prices are determined by a constant-product formula — the more players buy a resource, the higher its price climbs. The more they sell, the lower it drops.

The buy price is what you pay in gold to acquire one unit. The sell price is what you receive for selling one unit. The difference is the spread — a natural gold sink that keeps the economy healthy.

Prices on this page update hourly from automated telemetry snapshots.
