Blockchain

Every gold coin, resource, and crafted item in Full Circle MUD exists as a real asset on the XRP Ledger.

You own your loot. The economy is powered by XRPL's native AMM pools — prices are driven by real supply and demand, not scripts. From beta, you'll be able to export anything to your own wallet, trade it on-ledger, or import it back into the game.

The game is currently in pre-alpha on the XRPL mainnet, operating as a closed-loop system. Asset export is disabled until beta.

{# ── Xaman Wallet ── #}

Xaman Wallet

You'll need Xaman (formerly Xumm) to interact with the blockchain. Xaman is a self-custodial XRPL wallet available on iOS and Android. See our Xaman setup guide for installation and setup instructions.

Xaman is used for:

  • Logging in — sign a challenge to prove wallet ownership (no passwords)
  • Importing and exporting — sign transactions to move assets between your wallet and the game
  • Setting up trustlines — approve new currency types when you first export them

Your wallet address is your identity. The game never sees your private keys — every transaction is signed on your device in Xaman and verified on-chain.

{# ── On-Chain Assets ── #}

On-Chain Assets

All game assets are issued on XRPL by the Issuer wallet. There are three types:

FCMGold

The primary game currency, issued on the XRP Ledger by the Issuer wallet. During pre-alpha and alpha, the game operates in closed-loop mode — gold cannot be exported.

Resource Tokens

Over 50 XRPL issued currencies representing every harvestable and craftable resource in the game — Wheat, Iron Ore, Leather, Cloth, Moonpetal Essence, and more. Each resource is a separate issued currency on XRPL.

NFTs

Crafted items — weapons, armour, jewellery — are minted as XRPL NFTokens. Each NFT carries metadata describing the item's stats, enchantments, and crafting history. When you export an NFT, the actual game item transfers to your wallet.

{# ── AMM Pools ── #}

AMM Pools & Pricing

NPC shop prices are not scripted — they're driven by real on-ledger Automated Market Maker pools using XRPL's native AMM feature.

Each tradeable resource has a Gold ↔ Resource liquidity pool. Prices follow the constant product formula (x × y = k), meaning supply and demand directly determine what you pay. Buy more of a resource and the price goes up. Sell it back and the price drops.

  • Buy prices round up (slightly unfavourable to the buyer)
  • Sell prices round down (slightly unfavourable to the seller)
  • The spread between buy and sell goes to the liquidity pool

You can see live pool prices on the Markets page.

{# ── Trustlines ── #}

Trustlines

XRPL requires a trustline before your wallet can hold an issued currency. Think of it as telling the ledger "I'm willing to hold this token."

You don't need to set up trustlines in advance. The game handles it automatically: the first time you export a currency you don't have a trustline for, the game will send a TrustSet request to your Xaman wallet. Approve it, and the export proceeds. It's a one-time step per currency — once the trustline exists, future exports of that game token go through instantly.

The flow looks like this:

  1. You run export wheat 50 in the game
  2. The game checks your wallet — no trustline for FCMWheat yet
  3. The game sends you a TrustSet request via a Xaman link/QR code — approve it
  4. Trustline created — the export continues automatically
  5. Next time you export Wheat, it goes straight through
{# ── Import & Export ── #}

Import & Export

Note: Import and export are disabled during pre-alpha and alpha while the game operates in closed-loop mode. These features will be enabled at beta.

Moving assets between the game and your wallet is done with the import and export commands from the account view (out of character). All transactions are signed in Xaman — the game never has access to your private keys.

Export (Game → Wallet)

Take assets out of your in-game bank account and send them to your XRPL wallet.

  • Gold & resources: export gold 100 or export wheat 50 — the game vault sends a Payment to your wallet
  • NFTs: export <item> — the vault creates a sell offer, you accept it in Xaman, and the NFT transfers to your wallet
Import (Wallet → Game)

Send assets from your XRPL wallet back into the game.

  • Gold & resources: import gold 100 or import wheat 50 — you sign a Payment to the vault in Xaman, and the game credits your account
  • NFTs: use wallet to see your on-chain NFTs, then import <item> — you create a sell offer in Xaman, the vault accepts it, and the item appears in your bank

Every transaction is logged with its XRPL transaction hash for full auditability and crash recovery.
