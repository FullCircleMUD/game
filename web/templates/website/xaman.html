Xaman Wallet Setup

Set up your Xaman wallet to play Full Circle MUD.

Xaman is the mobile wallet you'll use to sign transactions on the XRP Ledger.

{# ── What is Xaman? ── #}

What is Xaman?

Xaman (formerly XUMM) is a mobile wallet for the XRP Ledger. It lets you securely sign transactions without sharing your private keys with anyone — including us.

Full Circle MUD uses Xaman for all on-chain interactions: paying your subscription, receiving items exported from the game, and managing your on-chain assets. When the game needs you to sign a transaction, it sends a request to your Xaman app — you review the details and approve it on your phone.

You'll need Xaman installed and set up before you can subscribe or use any blockchain features in-game.

{# ── Step 1: Install ── #}

Step 1 — Install Xaman

Download Xaman from your device's app store:

  • iOS: Search for Xaman in the App Store, or visit Xaman on the App Store
  • Android: Search for Xaman in the Google Play Store, or visit Xaman on Google Play

Xaman is free. Only download it from the official app stores — never from third-party links.

{# ── Step 2: Create Wallet ── #}

Step 2 — Create Your Wallet

  1. Open Xaman and tap Create a new account
  2. Choose your security method (biometrics or passcode)
  3. Xaman will generate your secret numbers — a set of 8 rows of 6 digits each
  4. Write these down on paper and store them safely. These are your only backup. If you lose them, nobody can recover your wallet — not Xaman, not us, not anyone.
  5. Confirm your secret numbers when prompted

Once complete, your wallet is ready. You'll see your r-address on the main screen — this is your public wallet address that you'll use in-game.

Your wallet needs a small XRP reserve (~1 XRP on mainnet) to activate on the ledger. This happens automatically when you receive your first XRP deposit.

{# ── Step 3: Link to Game ── #}

Step 3 — Link to Your Game Account

  1. Open the Full Circle MUD client and type connect
  2. The game will send a signing request to your Xaman app
  3. Open Xaman on your phone and approve the request
  4. Your wallet is now linked and you're connected to the game

Once linked, the game knows which wallet belongs to you. Subscription payments, item exports, and all other on-chain interactions will be sent to your Xaman app for signing.
