# FullCircleMUD

A text-based multiplayer online game (MUD) built on [Evennia](https://www.evennia.com/) with a transparent, player-driven economy on the [XRP Ledger](https://xrpl.org/).

## What Is This?

FullCircleMUD is a modern MUD in the tradition of CircleMUD and DikuMUD — deep, text-driven, imagination-first. An expanding roster of races and classes, over twenty weapon types with unique mastery progressions, crafting supply chains, procedural dungeons, and AI-driven NPCs that remember your name.

Gold and resources are issued currencies on the XRPL. Crafted items are NFTs. NPC shop prices are driven by on-chain AMM pools — supply and demand, not scripts. Everything you earn is yours, stored on a public blockchain.

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** [Evennia](https://www.evennia.com/) (Python/Django)
- **Blockchain:** XRP Ledger (XRPL)
- **AI:** LLM-powered NPCs via OpenRouter
- **Auth:** Xaman wallet sign-in (no passwords)
